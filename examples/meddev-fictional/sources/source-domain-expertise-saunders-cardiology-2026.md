# Domain Expertise: Saunders, 20 Years of Cardiology Workflow

## Summary

The founder's documented domain expertise: 20 years of direct exposure to cardiology workflows in operating rooms and outpatient clinics. Informs many planning-level claims that do not have customer-interview or market-data backing.

## Key Claims

- Clinical workflows in cardiology favor consistency over feature breadth.
- Cardiologists are conservative about new monitoring technology; trust is earned through workflow fit, not feature lists.
- Patients respond to "this is part of your prescribed care" framing far better than to "this is a wellness device."

## Interpretation

Domain expertise is a legitimate source of decisions in early-stage product work. Several pillars and decisions in this vault are anchored here because external evidence does not yet exist — but the founder's experience justifies the call.
