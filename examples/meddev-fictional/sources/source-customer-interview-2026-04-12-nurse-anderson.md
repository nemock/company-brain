# Customer Interview: Nurse Anderson (Northstar Cardiology, 2026-04-12)

## Summary

45-minute interview with the nurse coordinator at Northstar Cardiology who manages the practice's MCT cohort. Covers pad-change workflow, clinician-app friction with CardioTrace, and what the nurse-coordinator's daily experience is like.

## Key Claims (from the interview)

- Patients call the clinic an average of ~1.3 times per monitoring episode about pad issues (CardioTrace baseline).
- The clinician app's lack of EHR push is a daily friction point — they re-key data manually.
- The nurse coordinator believes a 7-day cycle is "easier to remember" than 14-day for both staff and patients.
- Pediatric patients are not in their workflow at all; they refer out.

This interview is one input among several. It is the strongest single evidence point for our user requirement around self-service pad replacement.
