# Strategic Thesis: Disposable Pad as Recurring Revenue Engine

## Summary

We believe a recurring pad business is the dominant lever for long-term defensibility in MCT. The wearable is the capital good that creates the pad-subscription relationship.

## Key Claims

- Recurring-revenue businesses are valued higher than one-time-hardware businesses by an order of magnitude.
- Recurring-pad customers have strong inertia — once a clinic is set up with our wearable, pad procurement is the path of least resistance.
- Competitors who lead with hardware lock-in face a different competitive dynamic than competitors who lead with pad-quality.

## Interpretation

Several decisions and pillars stand on this thesis. If the thesis weakens (e.g., pad commoditization happens faster than we expect), revisit the dependent decisions.
