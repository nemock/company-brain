# Question: When to Pursue Pediatric Clearance?

## Summary

The non-pediatric pillar is a current operating commitment, not a final answer. Under what conditions should we revisit it?

## Why This Matters

If we revisit too soon, we dilute focus. If we revisit too late, we miss a strategic opening.

## What Would Answer It

- A strategic partnership with a pediatric cardiology center that makes clinical data tractable.
- A material change in pediatric ambulatory monitoring reimbursement.
- Public pediatric-cardiac-monitoring market signals (competitor moves, payer policy shifts).
- A clear v3+ roadmap slot where pediatric is the next adjacent expansion.

## Edges

The intentional design is: the question and the pillar are mutually visible. An agent asked "should we explore pediatric?" should retrieve both — the current commitment AND the conditions that would flip it.

## Notes

Open. No expected answer date. The question staying open is healthy; closing it prematurely is the failure mode.
