Vitalisens Cardio

We serve adult cardiac patients in ambulatory settings during the 7- to 30-day window after a cardiac event or during arrhythmia workup.

Who it's for

Persona: Ambulatory Cardiac Patient — Adult patient discharged from cardiology service with continuous-monitoring orders for 7-30 days; mobile, lives at home, manages own pad changes. [persona-ambulatory-cardiac-patient]

What it does

Adult ambulatory cardiac telemetry wearable; continuous ECG capture, Bluetooth uplink to clinician app, 7-day pad cycle. [product-vitalisens-cardio]

Key feature: Feature: Continuous Dual-Channel ECG Recording [feature-continuous-ecg-recording] Vitalisens Cardio continuously records dual-channel ECG throughout the monitoring window, with motion artifact rejection via accelerometer.
