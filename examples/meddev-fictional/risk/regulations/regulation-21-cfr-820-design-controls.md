# Regulation: 21 CFR 820 (Quality System Regulation / Design Controls)

## Summary

The US FDA regulation establishing quality system requirements for medical device manufacturers, including the design controls subpart that governs design history files, risk management, and traceability.

## Content

Vitalisens' positioning of company-brain content as *planning, not controlled* is informed directly by 21 CFR 820. The controlled-document boundary documented at `docs/controlled-document-boundary.md` exists precisely to keep planning artifacts out of the regulatory regime that 820 creates.

This node is a *reference* — Vitalisens conforms to the regulation as part of its QMS, but the regulation itself is not authored or modified by us.
