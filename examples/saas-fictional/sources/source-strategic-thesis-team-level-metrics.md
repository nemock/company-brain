# Strategic Thesis: Team-Level Metrics Over Individual-Contributor Metrics

## Summary

Our bet on the right unit of analysis for engineering productivity.

## Content

Every engineering productivity tool that has tried to score individual contributors has hit the same wall: developers resist, leadership uses the data poorly, and the tool becomes politically toxic. The data may exist; using it well is a cultural and managerial problem the tool cannot solve.

We are betting that team-level signal — cycle time at the team level, deployment frequency at the team level, planning accuracy at the team level — is the right altitude. It maps to how VPEs already think about engineering. It does not invite the surveillance pattern that has killed the IC-level approach.

This thesis grounds [`pillar-team-level-signal`](../pillars/pillar-team-level-signal.md) and [`pillar-no-developer-surveillance`](../pillars/pillar-no-developer-surveillance.md).
