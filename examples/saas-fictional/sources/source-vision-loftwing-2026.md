# Founder Vision: Engineering Velocity is a Board-Level Conversation (Loftwing, 2026)

## Summary

The founder's thesis driving Loftwing.

## Content

Five years ago, engineering velocity was a metric the eng manager owned, the VPE looked at quarterly, and the board never heard about. That has shifted. Series A through C boards now ask "how fast is the engineering team shipping?" in every board meeting. The VPE is on the hot seat. And the tools they have to answer the question — Jellyfish, LinearB, Code Climate Velocity — were designed for engineering managers, not for the VPE communicating up to a non-technical board.

Loftwing is built for that conversation. The primary user is the VPE. The primary user-of-the-output is the board. The success criterion is "the VPE walks into a board meeting with a one-screen story that holds up to non-technical scrutiny." That is a different product than what the incumbents ship.
