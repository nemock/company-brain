# Domain Expertise: 12 Years of Engineering Management

## Summary

Founder's first-person experience as the target user.

## Content

The founder spent 12 years as an engineering manager and VPE at three B2B SaaS companies (one early-stage, two scale-ups). The product roadmap and the pillar set are downstream of that experience. When a customer says "we need X," the founder usually has a strong prior on whether X is real or pattern-matching to a half-remembered tool.

This is a vision-flavored source: it grounds claims that have no external evidence beyond "we have seen this play out repeatedly." The MRD's evidence-vs-vision split should flag domain-expertise-derived claims as vision, not evidence — they are bets, not measurements.
