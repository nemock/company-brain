# Non-Goal: No Consumer Market

## Summary

B2B only.

## What This Excludes

- **No individual-developer plans.** A solo developer cannot buy Loftwing for their own GitHub account.
- **No "free for personal projects" tier.** Distracts the team, doesn't fit the ICP, and creates churn-bait support load.
- **No App Store / consumer marketplace listings.** Discovery happens through B2B channels (peer recommendation, content, conferences) — not through consumer app stores.
- **No marketing positioning that addresses individual developers.** Our copy speaks to engineering leadership.

## Why this matters

It would be tempting to add a "$10/mo individual plan" to fill the funnel. The cost would be operationalising support, billing edge cases, and content for a segment we cannot serve well. This pillar firewalls that decision.
