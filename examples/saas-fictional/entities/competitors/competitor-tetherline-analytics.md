# Tetherline Analytics

## Summary

Adjacent fictional competitor in the enterprise tier we explicitly don't serve.

## Positioning

"Engineering analytics for the global enterprise." Contact-sales-only. Six-figure annual contracts. Heavy services attach (implementation engineers).

## Why we list them anyway

They are the competitor an enterprise procurement team will name when comparing options. When a customer at the edge of our ICP (200+ engineers) is evaluating, Tetherline often comes up. The right talking point: "you don't need that; here's why" — grounded in our pillars.

## Notes

We explicitly do NOT compete with Tetherline on enterprise feature parity. They have features (SSO required, audit trails for SOX compliance, per-user RBAC) that our PLG motion would not touch. That is by design.
