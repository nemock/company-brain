# Stakeholder: Founders

## Summary

Two co-founders.

## Roles

- **CEO** — product strategy, customer development, pricing, GTM, marketing. Primary author of pillars and decisions.
- **CTO** — engineering, infrastructure, integrations, hiring engineers. Final call on technical decisions.

## Decision rights

Pillars and decisions are co-authored by default. The CEO has tiebreaker authority on product and GTM; the CTO on engineering and infrastructure.
