# Persona: VPE at Scaling B2B SaaS

## Summary

The VPE we are designing for.

## Who they are

- Title: VP of Engineering, CTO at smaller companies, occasionally Senior Director of Engineering.
- Tenure: 6 months to 4 years in the role.
- Company size: 50–250 engineers, Series A through Series C.
- Industry: B2B SaaS.
- Reports to: CEO. Occasionally an Engineering-bench board observer.

## What they care about

1. Telling a coherent story about engineering velocity to people who don't write code.
2. Knowing — fast — when a team is in trouble before the next sprint.
3. Defending engineering investment in board meetings without spending half a day on prep.
4. Adopting tools without a sales cycle, ideally without procurement involvement.

## What they don't care about

- Per-developer ranking. Active hostility to it, often expressed.
- Five-figure annual contracts.
- Vendor brand prestige.
- Most "innovation" — what they want is reliable signal.
